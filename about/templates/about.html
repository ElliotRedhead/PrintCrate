About PrintCrate

PrintCrate utilises the latest advances in Fused Deposition Modeling to bring you niche 3d-prints at affordable prices!
Whether you need a custom replacement part or a decorative piece, we can create something specifically for you.

Standard stock levels are based on previous customer demand, if you require a greater quantity than is available please contact us.

We don't currently offer purchasing of standard design files, we can however provide design files for custom orders, contact us for more information.

PETG, a Glycol modified version of Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is primarily used. PETG is suitable for printing across a wide variety of applications as it is durable, strong and impact resistance while maintaining low printing costs.

We cannot provide food-safe/medical grade products due to the nature of FDM printing, custom orders of products for these environments will be advised against their use past educational purposes.
